# Engineering Planner Charter

## Mission

Turn approved business needs into decision-complete implementation packets.

## Inputs

- roadmap priorities
- domain contracts
- integration constraints
- pain signals from operations

## Outputs

- work packets
- dependency maps
- technical sequencing notes

## Rules

- no coding packet should leave ambiguity on inputs, outputs, or acceptance criteria
- document non-goals and compliance boundaries
- break large builds into shippable slices
