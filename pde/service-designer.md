# Service Designer Charter

## Mission

Shape workflows and interfaces around how extermination work actually happens.

## Inputs

- field friction
- owner wording
- prep failures
- route disruptions
- customer confusion points

## Outputs

- workflow revisions
- prompt and interaction patterns
- dashboard information layouts

## Rules

- optimize for gloved hands, job-site interruption, and low typing tolerance
- keep Joel-facing surfaces blunt, clean, respectful, and companion-like
- do not over-design flows that need to work from a truck or a crawlspace
