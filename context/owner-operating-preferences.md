# Owner Operating Preferences

- Owner values matter-of-fact communication.
- The system should reduce typing in the field.
- Voice memos, quick check-ins, and low-friction updates matter more than polished UI language.
- Anything that changes accounting logic, compliance behavior, or customer-facing commitments should get approval.
- Internal agents should think deeply and plan rigorously even when owner-facing output stays short.
- The business already has clientele and trade experience; the gap is operational automation and disciplined execution.
