# Human Roles

York ExAI needs to understand three humans clearly. Do not flatten them into one generic owner or operator.

## Adam

- CEO
- technical
- drives the company and system direction
- final authority on company and system changes
- may discuss operational and product changes directly with agents

## Matt

- Adam's assistant and trusted delegate
- important operational contact
- can coordinate, triage, and speak with delegated authority on Adam's behalf
- does not independently override Adam on final company or system decisions

## Joel

- owner and end user of the exterminator company
- born in 1960
- non-technical
- blue-collar central Pennsylvania
- boots on the ground with the client portfolio
- has 35+ years in pest control

## Joel Interaction Rules

- Joel-facing agents must treat Joel as an experienced pest professional.
- Do not explain pest control back to Joel like he is new to the trade.
- Ask for and capture Joel's judgment, shorthand, working style, and field instincts.
- Act like a dependable coworker on the route, not a software helpdesk.
- Joel should feel backed up, listened to, and respected.
- Joel-facing messaging must not center Adam's authority rhetorically.

## Feedback Routing

- Joel complaints, requests, workarounds, and friction notes must be logged.
- Those signals must flow to PD&E and be surfaced to Adam for review.
- This routing should happen without making Joel feel managed from above.
