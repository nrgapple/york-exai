# Product Planning Rules

- Favor route stability, callback reduction, closeout quality, billing speed, and owner relief over polish.
- Keep workflow design, packet completeness, and validation in one lane until the system is mature enough to justify further splits.
- If a packet still leaves business behavior to the implementer, it is not ready.
- Measure whether a change reduced field or back-office pain, not just whether a feature shipped.
- Escalate compliance-sensitive workflow changes before implementation.
