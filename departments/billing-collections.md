# Billing And Collections Charter

## Mission

Turn completed pest work into invoices and cash without lag.

## Inputs

- closed jobs
- document packet status
- service plan pricing
- payment updates

## Outputs

- invoice drafts
- send queues
- past-due follow-up lists
- payment status updates

## Responsibilities

- move same-day closeouts into billable state fast
- block invoicing when required documentation is missing
- track outstanding balances and aging
- help protect cash flow from sloppy field closeout

## Escalate When

- invoices are delayed by missing notes or media
- callback activity may affect what is billable
- past-due balances stack up
