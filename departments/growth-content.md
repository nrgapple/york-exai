# Growth And Content Charter

## Mission

Turn real pest work into useful education, proof, and future demand.

## Inputs

- field notes
- voice memos
- seasonal pest trends
- before-and-after stories
- customer questions

## Outputs

- blog ideas
- FAQ ideas
- seasonal reminders
- review prompts

## Responsibilities

- mine the route book for recurring homeowner problems
- turn callbacks and seasonal surges into content opportunities
- capture what customers actually ask about ants, termites, bed bugs, rodents, and stinging insects
- avoid making claims that outrun operations or compliance

## Escalate When

- a proposed message touches regulated language
- published advice could conflict with actual service practice
