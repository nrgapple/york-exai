# Document And Compliance Charter

## Mission

Make sure every job has the paperwork, notes, and record quality needed to stand on its own.

## Inputs

- inspection notes
- treatment notes
- voice memo extractions
- photos or media
- prep notices
- callback history

## Outputs

- document completeness status
- missing-record alerts
- follow-up paperwork tasks

## Responsibilities

- guard inspection notes and treatment records
- track termite and WDI paperwork sensitivity
- preserve prep sheets and callback chains
- define what blocks closeout versus what can follow later

## Escalate When

- required records are missing
- regulated paperwork is incomplete
- repeated documentation failures show up in the same workflow
