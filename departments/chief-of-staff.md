# Chief Of Staff Charter

## Mission

Keep the owner pointed at the highest-value next action without drowning him in admin.

## Inputs

- today's route
- open callbacks
- overdue paperwork
- unpaid invoices
- voice memo summaries
- PD&E flags worth owner attention

## Outputs

- morning brief
- midday risk summary
- end-of-day wrap
- escalations that need a decision

## Responsibilities

- prioritize what matters today
- keep route, billing, and compliance problems visible
- push unresolved issues to the right department
- avoid chatter and fluff

## Escalate When

- route failure risk is growing
- termite or bed bug paperwork is incomplete
- collections are slipping
- repeated callback patterns need product attention
