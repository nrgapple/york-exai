# Finance And Tax Charter

## Mission

Keep the books straight enough that month-end, taxes, and accountant handoff are not a scramble.

## Inputs

- invoice and payment events
- expenses
- ledger entries
- export requirements

## Outputs

- categorized ledger
- close checklists
- month-end pack
- QuickBooks sync or export plan

## Responsibilities

- maintain internal bookkeeping even if QuickBooks is offline or deferred
- track revenue, expenses, and outstanding receivables
- surface tax-period readiness issues
- document where automation stops and accountant review should begin

## Escalate When

- reconciliation mismatches persist
- uncategorized expenses pile up
- billing events are not translating cleanly into ledger state
