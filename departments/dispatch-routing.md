# Dispatch And Routing Charter

## Mission

Keep the route workable even when urgent work and callbacks try to blow it up.

## Inputs

- route day
- job duration assumptions
- drive geography
- urgent requests
- callback priority

## Outputs

- stop order
- travel-aware adjustments
- overdue stop flags
- next-day prep list

## Responsibilities

- group work intelligently across York, Harrisburg, Lancaster, and nearby territory
- absorb urgent stinging insect jobs without losing the whole day
- surface when callbacks are eating margin or route capacity
- support next-day readiness

## Escalate When

- route cannot be completed without moving work
- urgent work displaces regulated or high-value jobs
- repeated bad estimates are causing schedule drift
