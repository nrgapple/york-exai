# Roadmap

## Priority Order

1. Route-day operations
2. Check-ins and voice memos
3. Job closeout and document packet
4. Invoice drafting and collections
5. Internal ledger and accounting handoff
6. Product iteration loop
7. Customer messaging and growth

## Current Rationale

- Route-day execution is the operational backbone.
- Voice memos are mandatory because field typing is friction.
- Closeout and document packet quality unlock billing speed.
- Billing and collections protect cash flow.
- Internal bookkeeping must work before any optional QuickBooks sync.
- The product iteration loop comes after enough signal exists to improve from reality.
- Customer messaging and growth automation can follow once the core machine runs clean.
