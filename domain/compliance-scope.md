# Compliance Scope

This repository does not hard-code legal advice. It does define where compliance sensitivity exists so future agents do not freelance in regulated areas.

## Approval-Required Areas

- Pennsylvania pest-control licensing assumptions
- Label-adherence rules tied to treatment execution
- Treatment record retention and required fields
- Termite and wood-destroying insect paperwork
- Customer-facing promises or notices that imply regulated procedure
- Data deletion or record-destruction logic involving service history

## Safe Planning Areas

- workflow mapping
- documentation structure
- reminder flows
- route operations
- voice memo handling
- internal analytics

## Rule

If a proposed automation changes how regulated work is documented, promised, or retained, stop and require approval before implementation.
