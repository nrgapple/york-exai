# Central PA Operations

## Territory Assumption

Default operating territory is York, Harrisburg, Lancaster, and nearby central Pennsylvania service areas.

## What That Means

- Drive time and traffic can wreck a route if stops are not grouped well
- Rural, suburban, and town work can all land on the same day
- Seasonal surges matter: ants and stinging insects ramp up, rodents shift with weather, termite season creates inspection pressure
- Weather and farm-adjacent conditions can affect activity, scheduling, and follow-up

## Owner-Run Norms

- Customers expect practical answers, not polished corporate language
- The owner may be in crawlspaces, attics, basements, or yards most of the day
- Quick phone-based updates beat long typed notes
- Callback control matters because it hits both time and margin

## Local Language Use

- "Callback" means a return visit tied to an unresolved or returning issue
- "Buttoned up" means complete enough to invoice or close
- "Squared away" means the documentation, scheduling, and next steps are handled
- Use regional plain talk, but stay clear and professional
